About Me

Hi! My name is Hinal. I am a Python Data Scientist with a PhD in computational biology (officially “Medical Science”) from the University of Cambridge. I developed and optimized computational methods for visualizing and analyzing DNA sequencing data using PERL, Matlab, and R. Click here to read my thesis abstract. My other degrees are a Master of Research in Stem Cell Biology (University of Cambridge), and a Master in Science in Human Genetics (University of Nottingham).

As a Medical Writer for a healthcare consultancy, I combined my research, project management, data visualization and communication skills to assist clients in producing high-quality, peer-reviewed manuscripts, congress abstracts, posters, and oral presentations. I was in contact with clients and their stakeholders on a daily basis through calls and emails. By the time I was promoted to Senior Medical Writer, I was managing the company’s main client’s largest publication account, alongside multiple other projects. For my full work and education history, check out my LinkedIn profile.

I chose to quit medical writing in 2019 to re-train and pivot my career back towards more analytical and technical roles using online data science and machine learning courses and self-directed projects.

During my career break I also did a road trip around Scotland, spent 2 months in Washington DC learning about American history and culture, got two cats, managed a house renovation, did two 10-day silent meditation courses to start and maintain a regular meditation practice, created an arts studio in my garage and re-established a regular art and creativity practice.
